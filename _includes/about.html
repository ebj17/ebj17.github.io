About Me

I'm a mechanical engineer with a passion for building things that work — from precision CAD assemblies to custom PCBs and embedded systems. I bridge the gap between concept and physical reality using a combination of rigorous design practices and hands-on prototyping.

My background spans 3D modeling across industry-leading CAD platforms, rapid prototyping (FDM, SLA, CNC, laser cutting), and PCB design. I apply structured problem-solving frameworks like DFMEA and root cause analysis to ship reliable, manufacturable products.

I'm driven by the challenge of turning a blank canvas into something tangible. Whether it's a complex enclosure that needs to hit tight tolerances or a circuit board that needs to survive harsh conditions, I enjoy every step of the engineering process.
